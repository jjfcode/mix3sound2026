Who We Are

Mix3 Sound, Inc. is a premier live sound production company specializing in concert tours and corporate events throughout the United States. With years of industry experience, we've built a reputation for delivering exceptional audio quality and reliable service.

Our team of certified audio engineers and technicians is passionate about creating unforgettable sonic experiences. Whether it's a sold-out arena tour or an intimate corporate gathering, we bring the same level of professionalism and attention to detail to every project.
