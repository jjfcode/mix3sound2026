Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for tours and major events. However, we can often accommodate shorter timelines for corporate events and smaller productions.

Do you travel nationwide?

Yes, we provide services throughout the United States and can coordinate logistics for multi-city tours and events.

What brands of equipment do you use?

We use industry-leading brands including d&b audiotechnik, L-Acoustics, Meyer Sound, DiGiCo, Yamaha, Shure, and Sennheiser.

Do you offer equipment-only rentals?

Yes, we offer dry hire options for qualified audio engineers who need equipment only.
